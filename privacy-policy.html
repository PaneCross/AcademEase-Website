Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

AcademEase is committed to safeguarding the privacy of all users who interact with our website and services. This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, and protection of personal information you provide when using our website at academeasetransport.com.

1. Information We Collect

We gather information that you voluntarily submit via forms on our website. This may include:

  • Name
  • Area or location
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Notes or messages you include in form submissions

All other website content is provided for informational purposes only. We do not automatically collect browsing data beyond what is standard for website analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through our website is used solely to respond to your inquiries and provide you with our transportation services. We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties except as strictly necessary for service delivery or as required by applicable law.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Retention: Personal information is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Deletion: Upon verification of your identity, you may request deletion of your personal information. Please note that certain data may be retained if required for contractual obligations, security purposes, or legal compliance.

4. Security Measures

AcademEase implements industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Access controls limiting who can view personal data
  • Regular security audits
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Patch management and data backups
  • Employee training on data handling
  • Incident response protocols

AcademEase disclaims all liability for direct, indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from your use of this website or our services.

6. User Rights and Consent

By using this website and submitting your information, you consent to the information practices described in this Privacy Policy. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
