What We Provide

What Your Family Can Expect

From your child's first ride to their daily routine, here's what we promise to every family we serve.

Trained & Screened Drivers

Every AcademEase driver undergoes a thorough background check, drug screening, and specialized training for transporting students with diverse needs — including students with disabilities and those in vulnerable situations. Driver credentials are continuously tracked and verified.

Consistent, Familiar Drivers

We believe consistency matters for students — especially those with IEPs, in foster care, or experiencing housing instability. We work hard to assign the same drivers to the same students, building familiarity and trust that makes every ride more comfortable.

Real-Time Notifications

Stay informed at every step of your child's journey. You'll receive automatic text or app notifications when the driver is en route, when they arrive at the pickup stop, and when your child has been safely dropped off at school.

Safe, Well-Maintained Vehicles

Our fleet is regularly inspected and maintained to the highest standards. All vehicles are equipped appropriately for the students they transport, including wheelchair accessibility and safety equipment as required by each student's individual transportation plan.

Direct Communication

Questions? Concerns? Changes to your schedule? Our team is reachable directly during business hours and our dispatch team is available during transportation windows. You'll always have a real person to speak with when you need support.

Special Accommodations

We accommodate medical equipment, adaptive seating, feeding tubes, mobility devices, and other special requirements as outlined in your child's IEP or transportation plan. Your child's comfort and safety needs are our priority — we work with your school team to ensure everything is in place.
